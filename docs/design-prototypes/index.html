02

Gate symbols

Canonical ANSI distinctive shapes, drawn as SVG — AND is a D, OR is a pointed shield, NOT is a triangle. The inversion bubble is one reusable token: NAND = AND + bubble, NOR = OR + bubble. Here each gate is shown idle (left) and live (right, output HIGH).

The teaching lever: the little circle always means "NOT". Once a learner reads it on the inverter, NAND and NOR are visibly "the gate you know, stamped with invert" — directly serving lessons 3 & 4.
